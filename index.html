Bears

Adult grizzly bears are incredibly fast. They can run-up to an incredible 40mph and will possibly break speed limits on many roads in some countries.

  • Scientific Name: Ursidae
  • Average height: 4ft
  • Average lifespan: 54.69 Years
  • Habitat: forests, mountains, tundra, deserts and grassy areas

Bears have large bodies, a long snout, paws with claws, shaggy hair, stocky legs, and a short tail. Even though bears are big and heavy, they can run very fast and are also good at climbing and swimming.
